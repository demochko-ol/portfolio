keyboard_arrow_down User accounts (7) £25 each per month

Each active user account is charge at £25 per month. These are charged at the beginning of each month and if a user is added mid-billing cycle then the money will be debit immediately from your remaining credit. If you have insufficient funds you will have to top-up your account prior to adding additional users.

Manage user accounts

keyboard_arrow_down Calls 1.5p per minute

Calls are billed a 1.5p per minute. Minutes are only counted when a call connects and we round all calls up the nearest minute.

keyboard_arrow_down Recording 0.05p per minute

Recorded calls are billed a 0.05p per minute and can be switched on and off on a campaign by campaign basis so you only have to record the campaigns that you feel necessary.

Call recordings use up your storage allowance and you’ll be notified if you reach the end of the storage available in your chosen plan.

keyboard_arrow_down Phone numbers (2) £1 a month per number

Phone numbers are charge at £1 per number per month. You can add/remove numbers or assign them to invidual campaigns at any time by visiting the My Numbers section of the settings page.

keyboard_arrow_down Cloud Storage Free 500GB (72GB Used)

Your cloud storage is used to store all call recordings as well as data that you have uploaded or created in dialbase including invoices and scripts.
